Our Story

About Aetheria University

Founded in 1962, Aetheria has grown from a small technical institute into a globally recognised, multi-disciplinary research university.

Our History

Six Decades of Impact

Aetheria University was established in 1962 by a visionary group of academics and industry leaders who believed that quality education could transform communities. Beginning with just 400 students and 4 departments, the university has expanded to become one of India's top-ranked institutions.

Today, Aetheria operates across a 200-acre campus featuring state-of-the-art laboratories, a central library with 1.2 million volumes, and modern student residences. Our 1,400 faculty members bring international expertise to every classroom.
